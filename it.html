User Experience Design [UX]

A user experience designer combines art, business, and engineering to design products in a given industry. A designer has a solid grasp of the methodologies & principles necessary to creating a pleasurable and ease of use experience for the user. They create personas, scenarios, sketches, sitemaps, paper prototypes and wireframes. Usability research is a vital aspect to the design process as well as understanding the visual design requirements. Project management becomes essential when leading a team or working with visual designers, developers, project managers, and stakeholders.

Information Architecture [IA]

Information Architecture is the art and science of organizing and labeling of information systems. An architect is skilled in developing visual representations of organizational structure, hierarchy, and relationships. They are proficient in creating the structural design of shared information environments such as intranets, online communities, software and websites. An architect understands the needs of a user and can collaborate with interaction designers, visual designers and developers.

User Interaction Design [UI]

A user interface designer is passionate about creating the best design and user experience. This is accomplished by focusing on the user interface and the overall feel of an application. They have a tendency to conceptualize designs and have no problem maintaining brand consistency across the product platform. UI designers collaborate with user researchers, interaction designers and developers.

User Experience Researcher

A user experience researcher can lead, plan and execute user research activities. They develop research protocols to screen material efficiently and gather user requirements. A researcher can employ the tools and methods necessary to recruit research participants through interviews and surveys. They use focus groups and can articulate concise and action-orientated research reports.

Web Designer

Changing the font type or working with Photoshop is a breeze for a designer. As a web designer you love creating visual concepts to communicate ideas that inspire, inform, or engage audiences. Designers combine art and technology to develop the overall layout and production design for templates and especially web pages. As a web designer, you work closely with your team, which could include front-end developers and marketing.

Visual Design

Visual Designers are responsible for creating captivating and innovative web-based and mobile products. They bring concepts to life using the Adobe Creative suite, some HTML/CSS and other graphic tools. They create product advertisements, brochures, illustrations, logos, newsletters and templates for web pages. Designers understand how to cultivate a bond between the customer and the brand.
